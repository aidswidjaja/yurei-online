HOW TO PLAY

  • To participate, use the official post-jam speedrun build available at http://heyo.adrian.id.au/yurei-online/index.html.
  • You can press the O (the letter "o") key to reset back to level 1
  • All speedruns are counted from level 1 to thank-you screen
  • When you reach the end, you'll see a screen with the number of seconds it took for you to complete the game (starting at level 1) - feel free to screenshot to show off to your friends
  • Submit your username and then click the button to submit your score here

HOW TO READ

  • The Score column can be ignored
  • The Text column contains the number of seconds from start of level 1 to thank-you screen, to a double-precision floating point
  • Times tagged with M were runs that were manually timed before this system got implemented

This game was originally submitted for Ludum Dare 49, but the speedrun build with support for online score submission was developed post-jam
